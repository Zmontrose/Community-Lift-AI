Executive Summary
CommunityLift AI provides free access to AI tools that help underserved individuals create
resumes, build personal brands, publish recovery stories, develop digital products, and connect
with grants and opportunities. The platform will remain open-source and publicly accessible.
Public Goods Justification
The platform benefits the public, provides open educational resources, and lowers barriers to
economic opportunity. Core functionality will be freely available.
Impact Goals
Year 1 Targets: 5,000 users served, 500 stories published, 250 digital businesses launched, 100
employment placements, and a growing mentor network.
